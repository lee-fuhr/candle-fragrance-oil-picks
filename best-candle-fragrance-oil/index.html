Choosing the right fragrance oil for soy wax can make or break your candles. Soy wax has a lower melt point than paraffin, so it needs oils with a lower flash point and a chemistry that binds well to the wax for a strong cold and hot throw. The best oils are concentrated, phthalate-free, and specifically formulated for soy, but not all blends are equal. We’ve researched owner feedback, published expert reviews, and reputation across the major candle-making suppliers to find the fragrance oils that deliver consistent performance, clear labeling, and reliable scent intensity.
